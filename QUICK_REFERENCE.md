## 🎨 HOW YOUR WEBSITE WORKS

### For BUYERS (Searchers):

```
1. Visit sachabroker.cm
2. Sign up as "Searcher"
3. Search for properties
   - By location (city, area, pincode)
   - By price
   - By size (BHK)
4. Click on property
5. See photos and details
6. Contact info is HIDDEN 🔒
7. Click "Unlock Contact" → Pay ₹299
8. NOW see phone number, email, WhatsApp ✅
9. Call seller directly
```

### For SELLERS (Listers):

```
1. Visit sachabroker.cm
2. Sign up as "Lister"
3. Click "Add Property"
4. Fill details:
   - Address
   - Price
   - Photos (1-10 images)
   - Description
5. Choose:
   - Free listing (basic)
   - OR Featured (₹499) - appears on top
6. Publish
7. Wait for buyers to unlock contact
8. Earn money when buyers unlock! 💰
   (You get notified by email)
```

## 🔒 SECURITY & RULES

### One Email = Two Accounts Possible:

**Example:**
- vamshi@gmail.com → Searcher account ✅
- vamshi@gmail.com → Lister account ✅ (Different account!)

**But NOT:**
- vamshi@gmail.com → Searcher account #1 ✅
- vamshi@gmail.com → Searcher account #2 ❌ (Not allowed)

### Searchers CANNOT:
- ❌ List properties
- ❌ See other searchers
- ❌ Access lister features

### Listers CANNOT:
- ❌ Search properties
- ❌ See other listers' properties
- ❌ Access searcher features

**They are COMPLETELY SEPARATE!** 🚫
