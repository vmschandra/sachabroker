Refund Policy

Last Updated: February 12, 2026

Overview

At Sachabroker, we are committed to providing exceptional service to all our users. This refund policy outlines the circumstances under which refunds may be issued for services, listing fees, or premium memberships on our platform.

Service Fees and Listings

Property Listing Fees

If you have paid for a premium property listing or featured placement:

  • Refund Window: Full refund available within 48 hours of payment if listing has not been published
  • Partial Refund: 50% refund available within 7 days if listing performance does not meet guaranteed metrics
  • No Refund: After 7 days or if listing has received inquiries/views as promised

Premium Membership

For users who have subscribed to our premium membership:

  • Trial Period: Full refund available within 14 days of initial subscription
  • Annual Plans: Pro-rated refund available if canceling within first 30 days
  • Monthly Plans: No refund for current month, but you can cancel for future months

Important: All refund requests must be submitted through our official channels. Processing time is typically 5-10 business days.

Non-Refundable Services

The following services are non-refundable:

  • Completed transactions between buyers and sellers
  • Professional photography or virtual tour services after delivery
  • Background verification fees
  • Legal documentation services after documents are prepared
  • Third-party services arranged through our platform
  • Custom listing promotions after campaign launch

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  • Step 1: Log in to your account and navigate to "My Profile" → "Billing"
  • Step 2: Click on "Request Refund" for the eligible transaction
  • Step 3: Provide reason for refund request and supporting documentation
  • Step 4: Submit the form and await review (typically 2-3 business days)
  • Alternative: Contact our support team at refunds@sachabroker.com

Processing Time

Once your refund request is approved:

  • Credit card refunds: 5-10 business days
  • PayPal refunds: 2-5 business days
  • Bank transfers: 7-14 business days
  • Cryptocurrency refunds: 1-3 business days (subject to network fees)

You will receive an email confirmation once the refund has been processed. Please note that the time it takes for the refund to appear in your account may vary depending on your financial institution.

Chargebacks

We strongly encourage you to contact us directly to resolve any issues before initiating a chargeback with your bank or credit card company. Chargebacks may result in:

  • Suspension of your account pending investigation
  • Additional fees to cover chargeback processing costs
  • Permanent ban from the platform if chargeback is deemed fraudulent

Exceptions and Special Circumstances

We understand that unique situations may arise. Refund requests will be considered on a case-by-case basis for:

  • Technical issues preventing access to paid services
  • Duplicate charges or billing errors
  • Service outages affecting your listing visibility
  • Fraudulent activity on your account
  • Force majeure events affecting property transactions

Dispute Resolution

If your refund request is denied and you wish to dispute the decision:

  • Submit a formal dispute through our support portal within 30 days
  • Provide any additional evidence or documentation
  • Our dispute resolution team will review within 10 business days
  • Final decision will be communicated via email

Changes to This Policy

Sachabroker reserves the right to modify this refund policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to the website. We will notify users of significant changes via email and on-platform notifications. Your continued use of our services after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
